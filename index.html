ABOUT

Wannie is a singer-songwriter-producer behind EverLasting Music Productions. She was born and raised in the Philippines but currently resides in Seattle area, Washington. Her top three musical influences are Sara Bareilles, Ingrid Michaelson and Marit Larsen. Her sweet, gentle vocals invite you to listen to warm folk-pop/indie melodies that convey lighthearted, feel-good themes such as home and togetherness.
